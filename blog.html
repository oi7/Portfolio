Finalist, HackMIT

HackMIT September 20, 2015 Cambridge, Massachusetts

Best Team Award, MSRA Student Hackathon

Microsoft Research Asia September 20, 2015 Xi'an, China

bAd, best Advertisement, is a prototype of an interactive advertising wall using technologies from Microsoft, including Face API, Speech API of Project Oxford and Cortana.
• Team: Poseidon, Jason, Naiwen, Winston, Tony, Andy, Wang

1st Place, Best Innovation Award, Best Hardware Award, UniqueHackDay

UniqueHackDay June 7, 2015 Wuhan, China

CellPhone combines 4 components to transform your iPhone into a microscope, and use Microsoft Azure Machine Learning to train the model for tagging specific parts of an identified object.
• Team: Poseidon, Charlie, Jay, Wei

Pebble Technology Award, THacks

THacks November 23, 2014 Beijing, China

PebbleMario is a brand new experience of playing virtual games with Pebble Smart Watch and various sensors.
• Team: Poseidon, Kenny, Murray, Hao

3rd Place, Hardware Hack, HackShanghai

HackShanghai November 16, 2014 Shanghai, China

Olert is a wearable+app system that provides more effective protection with much less cost than other safeguard products.
• Team: Poseidon, Ran, Jerome, Zhijin

Accuvally Technology Award, HackNTU

HackNTU May 25, 2014 Taipei, Taiwan

ActivityBuzz is a personal activity assistant (web app) which analyzes your hehaviors (Share, Like, Comment) on social networks (Facebook) and pushes the most relevant activities to you based on categorized tags on Accupass.
• Team: Poseidon, Wei-chieh, Hsi-che, Sheng-yen
